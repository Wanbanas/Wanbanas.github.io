The Biggest Wearable Technology Trends In 2021

With the ongoing global pandemic, wearable technology didn’t make as many waves in 2020 as it has done in previous years. This isn’t surprising, as many wearable tech items are designed to be worn when we were out and about.

How Wearable Technology Will Change the Way We Live in the Future

Wearables have continued to influence our lives greatly, and there is so much to expect as the technology keeps on getting better in the future. Perhaps we are going to see the technology being applied in the fashion industry to express our identity, social status, and emotions, or being made as part of our body to increase our cognitive abilities.
